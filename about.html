Oral & Dental Surgeon

Dr. Md. Rezaul Karim Raki

  BDS · MPH · PGT (Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery) · Assistant Professor

Dr. Md. Rezaul Karim Raki is a highly qualified and dedicated Oral & Dental Surgeon with a strong academic background and advanced clinical training. He completed his BDS degree from the University of Rajshahi and further pursued his MPH from NIPSOM, Mohakhali. He holds the position of Assistant Professor at Udayan Dental College, Rajshahi, and completed his advanced Postgraduate Training (PGT) in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University (BSMMU), PG Hospital.

At মা ওরাল এন্ড ডেন্টাল কেয়ার, Manikdi Bazar, Dhaka Cantonment, Dr. Rezaul Karim Raki provides expert dental care with a patient-first approach — offering everything from routine checkups to complex oral surgical procedures in a comfortable and hygienic environment.
